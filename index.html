Health Insurance Risk Calculator

==========================================================================

This website is disigned to calculate your health insurance risk which will help determine how much your health insurance will cost. You will be asked a series of questions in which you will answer with the closest option shown in the drop down. This also includes a body mass index (BMI) calculator too.




These following two questions relating to height and weight will be for your BMI once these next two questions are answered press the Calculate BMI button to get your BMI result to know which BMI category you are in.












Below is a chart from the American Heart Association to help determine your blood pressure category. For this you will need your stystolic (upper number) and dystolic (lower number) numbers to be able to do this. If you do not know you can go to your local pharmacy to get your blood pressure taken.
